emNTWRK (Eddie Mohammad’s Network) is a language learning network that focuses on the creative development of human capital. Initiated in 2011 by Eddie Mohammad (Director) and Citra Riskomar (Principal), the network has provided language education services for students coming from various backgrounds.

Learning Purpose

We identify the enhancement of consumption, comprehension, and communication as the main purposes of language learning.

There is only one rule in learning, “to understand is to be able to explain” (paham berarti dapat menjelaskan).
